Oatmeal

A bowl of oatmal is simialar to a bowl of cereal. Oatmeal is fairly simple to make and doesnt require too much ingredients. LEarn to make yuourself a bowl of oatmeal that is nutricious and will give you energy to jumpstart your day

Ingredients

  • bowl
  • oats
  • water or milk
  • fruits

Steps

  1. pull out bowl
  2. pour oats into bowl
  3. pour milk or water into bowl
  4. warm bowl for up to two minutes
  5. let bowl sit and cool
  6. add bite-sized fruits
